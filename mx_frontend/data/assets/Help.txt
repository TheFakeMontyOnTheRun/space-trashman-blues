Place the EMP near the reactor control
valve to cause it to drop the fuel
rods and prevent the meltdown.

Make sure to take a safe distance!

Controls:

- S and D: strafe
- Z: use current item
- A: use current item on what in front
- X: drop current item
- C: pick or use whats in front of you
- V: toggle current item
- F: inspect current item
