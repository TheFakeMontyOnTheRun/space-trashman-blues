Place the EMP near the reactor control
valve to cause it to drop the fuel rods

Controls (besides the arrow keys):

S and D: strafe.

Z: if you have an object in front of
you, use what's your hand with it. 
If not, use what's in your hand.

X: If you can't pick the object in 
front of you, do it. If you can't, 
interact with it. If there's nothing, 
drop the object in your hand.

C: Inspect the object in your hand.

V: Next item in the inventory.
