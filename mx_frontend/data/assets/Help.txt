You must find the traitor and accuse
him or her once you find yourself on
the same city.

During interrogations, try to find the
red spot to find the truth - pay close
attention to their stress levels!

To succeed an infiltration, stay close
to the walls and watch for the guards.

Controls:
- arrows will move
- C to pick objects
- X to aim (before you shoot)
- Z to shoot (must aim first)
- S and D to strafe
